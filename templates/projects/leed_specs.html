LEED Certification Overview

Select a LEED category to view or upload related information:

Integrative Process (IP)

Details about the integrative design process used in the project.

Location and Transport (LT)

Information related to site accessibility, alternative transportation, etc.

Sustainable Sites (SS)

Site management, stormwater control, and outdoor environment.

Water Efficiency (WE)

Strategies for indoor/outdoor water use reduction.

Energy and Atmosphere (EA)

Energy performance, renewable systems, and commissioning.

Materials and Resources (MR)

Use of environmentally preferred materials and waste diversion.

Indoor Environmental Quality (EQ)

Ventilation, lighting, and occupant comfort metrics.

Innovation (IN)

Creative strategies that go beyond the standard LEED credits.

Regional Priority (RP)

Extra credits awarded based on local environmental priorities.

Equity and Resilience (ER)

Inclusion of equity-focused practices and climate resilience planning.

General Inspection / Admin

Documentation, progress tracking, and general administration.
